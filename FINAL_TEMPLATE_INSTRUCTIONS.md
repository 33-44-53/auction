### Step 2: Add Your Background/Logo

**Method 1: Add Watermark**
1. Click **Design** tab
2. Click **Watermark** → **Custom Watermark**
3. Select **Picture watermark**
4. Click **Select Picture** and choose your logo/background
5. Click **OK**

**Method 2: Add Background Image**
1. Click **Design** tab
2. Click **Page Color** → **Fill Effects**
3. Go to **Picture** tab
4. Click **Select Picture** and choose your background
5. Click **OK**

**Method 3: Insert Logo**
1. Click **Insert** tab
2. Click **Pictures**
3. Choose your logo file
4. Resize and position it (usually top-left or center)
5. Right-click → **Wrap Text** → **Behind Text** (to make it a background)
