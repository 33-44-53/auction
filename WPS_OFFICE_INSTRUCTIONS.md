## 🎨 How to Add Background in WPS Office

### Method 1: Add Watermark (Recommended)

1. **Open** `WINNER_LETTER_TEMPLATE_READY.docx` in WPS Office
2. Click **Insert** tab at the top
3. Click **Watermark** button
4. Select **Picture Watermark**
5. Click **Select Picture**
6. Choose your logo/background image
7. Adjust settings:
   - Scale: 100% or Auto
   - Washout: Uncheck if you want full color
8. Click **OK**

### Method 2: Add Background Image

1. **Open** `WINNER_LETTER_TEMPLATE_READY.docx` in WPS Office
2. Click **Page Layout** tab (or **Layout** tab)
3. Click **Page Color**
4. Select **Fill Effects**
5. Go to **Picture** tab
6. Click **Select Picture**
7. Choose your background image
8. Click **OK**

### Method 3: Insert Logo as Background

1. **Open** `WINNER_LETTER_TEMPLATE_READY.docx` in WPS Office
2. Click **Insert** tab
3. Click **Picture** → **Picture from File**
4. Select your logo/background image
5. After inserting:
   - Right-click on the image
   - Select **Wrap Text** → **Behind Text**
   - Resize and position as needed
   - You can also right-click → **Format Picture** → adjust transparency

### Method 4: Add Header/Footer Logo

1. **Open** `WINNER_LETTER_TEMPLATE_READY.docx` in WPS Office
2. Double-click at the top of the page (header area)
3. Click **Insert** → **Picture**
4. Select your logo
5. Position it where you want
6. Click outside the header to return to document
